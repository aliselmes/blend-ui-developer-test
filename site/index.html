How does Lunchpod work?

Lunchpod is the fastest and easiest way to pack lunches for your kids: always healthy, always fun, and always on your doorstep in time for the next school week. It's as easy as 1-2-3.

1

Sign Up

Parents create an account with password, method of payment and shipping address. Kids get their own portal to make their lunches and customize their LunchPod.

Get Started ›

2

Pick Your Stuff

The kid-friendly tool makes picking a main, side and sweet easy for kids for all ages.

View Food ›

3

Get the week's Pods

Each Pod arrives custom-labeled for the kid who picked it. Fresh food lasts all week in the fridge and all day in a backpack.

View Availability Map ›

4

Off you go

Take your personalized LunchPod from fridge to backpack and wow your friends at lunchtime. Complete nutrition and real food means you're powered up for the day.
